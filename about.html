About Me3

My name is Seth Wilson, and I am a high-performance coach, sales leader, and business owner from the Kansas City area.

In my current role, I lead sales professionals at Commenco, a technology solutions company. Commenco specializes in safety and security solutions, however, we are most known for helping our customers solve their challenges with relentless focus and attention to detail.

I am continuing to challenge myself through continued education as I work towards completing my MBA at the University of Missouri-Columbia, with an expected completion in 2026. My background includes a Bachelor of General Studies that focused on Business Management and Psychology curriculum, which was also achieved at the University of Missouri-Columbia. These educational experiences, combined with my time coaching elite-level athletes, have helped shape me into a passionate and team-oriented leader who values collaboration and perseverance.

I believe in the continued pursuit of excellence and, by leveraging my expertise in coaching and business leadership, I strive to help others unlock their full potential and achieve their biggest goals.
