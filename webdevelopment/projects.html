Experiences



Director, Sales & Marketing
Commenco

Description

In this role, I led all aspects of sales management, team development, and sales related initiatives that helped the team to exceed their individual and team targets. By leveraging data-driven strategies we successfully implemented new tools, sales methodologies, and incorporated data dashboards that helped our team succeed. Our team adopted Sandler sales methodologies and a new CRM platform to improve lead tracking and client relationship management. These improvements and managing what we measured contributed to record revenue growth. Additionally, our efforts secured record revenue with Motorola Solutions with a 170% YOY increase in sales. This increase allowed Commenco to finish as a top 20 partner in North America for the first time. In addition to that, by strategically restructuring the sales team staff, we saw a 30% increase in overall performance since 2021. This was also achieved by developing go-to-market plans for newly introduced product lines where our new processes created a 140% year-over-year increase in product category sales in 2023, reinforcing Commenco’s position as a market leader.

Reflection

This position has reinforced the importance of combining data-driven insights with strong coaching strategies. Guiding the team through the implementation of new processes taught me how to balance big-picture thinking with day-to-day tactics, ensuring consistent growth. Watching individual team members thrive and evolve under structured training has been a highlight of my career, and it continues to fuel my passion for leadership and innovation.
